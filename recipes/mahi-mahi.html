Mahi-Mahi

Description

This is a very simple recipe that yeilds a very tasty dish that is light and refreshing. The citrus from the lemon will go well with the parsely and basil, and some added garlic will give it an extra kick.

Ingredients:

  • 2 filets of Mahi-Mahi
  • 1 tbsp of olive oil
  • 1/2 tspn of parsley
  • 1/2 tspn of basil
  • 1/2 tspn of salt
  • 1/2 tspn of black pepper
  • 2 cloves of minced garlic
  • 2-4 thin slices of lemon

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. While the oven is preheating we will prep.
  2. Set the filets out and pat dry with paper towels. Mince 2 cloves of garlic, slice your lemon, and set aside.
  3. Mix together all the spices in a bowl. Brush with oil and cover both sides of the filets with the spice mix.
  4. Once the oven is preheated, set the filets on a lightly oiled baking sheet. Top with minced garlic and lemon slices and then put them in the oven for 12-15 minutes
  5. Pull them out of the oven after the time is up, remove the lemon slices, and serve with rice or vegetables!
